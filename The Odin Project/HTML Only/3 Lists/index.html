Fishball Quick SnackL

This is a page for a quick snack with fishball.

Buy the following:

  • Fishball
  • Suka
  • Chili Powder
  • Sago't Gulaman

Preparation:

  1. Get a bowl.
  2. Mix the suka and chili powder.
  3. Get another bowl and a fork.
  4. Sawsaw the fishball to the sawsawan bowl and enjoy eating. *smiley*
